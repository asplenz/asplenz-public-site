# About Asplenz

## Structured governance for teams that build with AI.

Asplenz builds tools that help organizations capture, enforce, and prove their decisions.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Teams make decisions every week. Most are never written down. The ones that are end up scattered across wikis, Slack threads, and people's heads.

When AI agents enter the workflow — writing code, reviewing PRs, making deployment decisions — they act without context about your team's rules. And when regulators ask "what governed this AI-generated output?", there's no structured answer.

Asplenz fixes this with two products:

### Knowledge
The decision registry for teams that ship with AI agents. Extract rules from existing docs. Record decisions with context. Enforce constraints in real time. Prove compliance automatically.

### Evidence
Verifiable proof artifacts for decisions with legal weight. Cryptographic seals, tamper-proof chains, and independent verification for regulated environments.

---

## Principles

**Human oversight, not human bottleneck.** AI agents should respect your rules automatically. Humans review and approve — they don't repeat checks that machines can do.

**Structure over documents.** Decisions in a searchable registry with typed relations are more useful than decisions in a markdown file no one reads.

**Prove, don't claim.** Every constraint check, every approval, every agent query produces a structured trace. Compliance is a database query, not a manual investigation.

**Start from what you have.** Your team already has rules. Automatic extraction surfaces them. You don't start from zero.
