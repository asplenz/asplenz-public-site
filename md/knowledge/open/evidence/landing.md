# Evidence

## Verifiable proof artifacts for decisions with legal weight.

Some decisions need more than a record — they need cryptographic proof that the decision was made, by whom, when, and that the evidence hasn't been tampered with.

Evidence produces independently verifiable proof artifacts for legally binding decisions.

---

## What Evidence Does

- **Seals decisions** into immutable, timestamped proof bundles with Ed25519 signatures
- **Chains proof** so every artifact references its predecessors — forming an unbreakable audit trail
- **Enables independent verification** — any third party can verify a proof artifact without access to your systems
- **Maps to regulatory requirements** — EU AI Act, SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR accountability

---

## Who It's For

- **Regulated industries** that need tamper-proof records of governance decisions
- **Legal and compliance teams** producing evidence for auditors and regulators
- **Organizations deploying high-risk AI** under the EU AI Act's documentation requirements
- **Any team** where "we decided X on date Y" must be provable, not just claimed

---

## Evidence and Knowledge

Evidence and Knowledge are independent products. Knowledge captures and enforces your team's decisions, rules, and constraints. Evidence produces cryptographic proof artifacts when decisions carry legal weight.

They can work together — or separately.
